title: Wine Industry
description: Simplifying the wine industry. 
---

This application consolidates information from suppliers, critics, blogs, forums, magazines and other sources into a single place. The knowledge collected allows us to model wines and wine preferences to allow end users to discover and find the best value wines suited to their tastes.

This module includes the following suppliers
- Dan Murphies
- My Cellars
- Kemenys
- VintageDirect
- WineboxWarehouse
- WineDirect
- WineStar
